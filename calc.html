Calculator

 

Instructions for use:

  • Numbers can be no more than 9 digits (or 8 digits and 1 decimal) long, additional number input beyond that specified will do nothing
  • Only 1 decimal point can be in any entered number, pressing it again will do nothing
  • After an operation is evaluated the resulting number can be immediately added, subtracted, multiplied, or divided by another number after clicking the desired operator
